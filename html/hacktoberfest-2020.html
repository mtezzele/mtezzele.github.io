How to start
  • [Be amazed] Go to the Hacktoberfest 2020 website and look at the wonderful Tee that can be yours or at the beautiful tree you can plant.

  • [Registration] Click START HACKING and follow the procedure (you need a GitHub account).

  • [Your profile] You can follow your progress in your profile page.

  • [Have fun!] Don't be stressed, enjoy the process, we are here to help you.
How to contribute to the mathLab packages
  • [GitHub page] You can find all our packages and library at the mathLab page.

  • [A gentle start] We set up a spreadsheet where you can find some easy tasks to start with.

  • [Issues] We suggest you to have a look at the ISSUES tab of the single packages.

  • [Be proactive] Do you have an idea to improve a package? Do you feel something is missing? We will help you to make it a reality.

Some treats
  • [Speedy Gonzales] Special prize for the first who complete 4 PRs starting from today (8 October 2020).

  • [The completist] Nothing is better than reading a comprehensive tutorial. Everyone who adds a new tutorial or improves an existing one will get a unique gift.

  • [The Doc(tor)] For those who "4 lines of documentation for every line of code". Complete a module docs to get this coveted prize.

  • [Ctrl+Z] For the ones who get stuck with conficts to solve. Unlimited availability, please DO NOT rush into this :)
